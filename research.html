Research

Our research focuses on understanding the unique barriers faced by refugees, non-EU residents, and those without traditional secondary education. By collaborating with students, educators, and community leaders, we gather valuable perspectives that inform the development of our platform.

Initial Research

Why?

Prototyping UnityHub transformed abstract ideas into a functional design blueprint. By focusing on inclusivity and accessibility, we ensured the platform aligned with our mission to serve underserved communities. This phase laid the foundation for a user-friendly and impactful platform.

Wireframing

We began with wireframes to outline UnityHub’s structure and user flow. These sketches balanced functionality and simplicity, enabling users to navigate effortlessly. Collaboration with designers refined these layouts into a solid framework.

Iterative Testing

Interactive prototypes simulated real-world use, allowing us to gather crucial feedback. Testing with our target audience revealed pain points and guided improvements. Iterative refinement ensured the platform met user needs effectively.

Finalizing the Prototype

The final prototype was polished for seamless development, with responsive layouts and consistent visuals. Collaborating with developers ensured scalability and performance, setting the stage for UnityHub’s success.
